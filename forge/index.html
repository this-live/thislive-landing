Forge · the adapter factory

Your own models, improving on your own work.

Forge is the factory that turns your fleet's own labels into local specialists — so you stop depending on a provider's model. The factory is built end-to-end and gated so it cannot lie: nothing gets promoted without passing an eval. The base model already serves on your own GPU.

The flywheel

Labels → harvest → curate → train → eval-gate → serve

01

Harvest + curate

8 lanes, a harvest loop on a non-Hermes cron, and curate gates with depth, safety, and alignment checks. Built end-to-end.

02

Cloud training harness

A Modal cloud execution harness, proven on a CPU end-to-end run at $0. The pipeline runs; the trained-adapter step is gated and pending.

03

Eval gate

An eval-gated promotion: a candidate must measurably beat the base before it can be served. Registry adapters stay planned until they pass.

04

Serve locally

The base model serves on your own GPU today. Routing local-first to a Forge adapter is the step that lights up once an adapter is trained and passes.
