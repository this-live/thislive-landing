Beacon Architecture: How AI Builds Warm Leads Automatically

By Bryce Murad · this.live

Beacon is my live SaaS product, and I want to explain how it actually works under the hood. Not the marketing version. The actual architecture. Because it is a good example of what agentic software looks like when you stop bolting chatbots onto old products and actually rethink what is possible.

Beacon finds small businesses that do not have websites. It scrapes public data: business listings, reviews, social media, whatever is available. Then it generates a rich prompt based on that context. The prompt describes the business, its services, its location, its customer feedback, its competitors. It is a comprehensive profile built from public information.

That prompt gets sent to an agent that builds a demo website. Not a template with the business name slapped on it. A genuinely customized site based on the actual business. Images, copy, layout, color scheme, all generated from the context. The agent writes HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It chooses fonts based on the industry. It writes copy based on the reviews. It selects images that match the vibe of the business.

Then Beacon sends that demo to the prospective client via SMS or email. The message is personalized. It references their actual reviews, their actual services, their actual location. And it ends with a simple question: do you want to see your new website?

This is not a cold call. It is a warm introduction with value already delivered. The business owner can see exactly what they would get before paying anything. They can click the link and see a website built specifically for their business. Not a generic pitch. A concrete demonstration of value.

Behind the scenes, this involves web scraping, LLM prompt engineering, code generation, image generation, SMS and email APIs, and a lead tracking system. It is a pipeline of agentic steps, each one automated, each one feeding into the next. Scraping generates context. Context generates a prompt. A prompt generates a website. A website generates a conversation. A conversation generates a customer.

Beacon is CRM plus maps API plus AI code generation equals never-ending warm leads. That is the kind of product that is only possible now. You could not have built this two years ago. The models were not good enough. The tools were not mature enough. The costs were too high.

Now they are. And that is why I am building it.

If you want to see what agentic software actually looks like, this is it. Not a chatbot. Not a widget. A fully autonomous pipeline that identifies prospects, creates value, and initiates conversations. While you sleep.

That is the future of sales. That is the future of service businesses. That is Beacon.
