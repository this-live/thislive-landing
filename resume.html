Bryce Murad — Founder & AI Operator

Bryce Murad is the founder of This.Live, a local-first AI software publishing house building products, infrastructure, and operating systems around agentic AI. His work sits at the intersection of software, networks, model systems, product strategy, and practical human judgment.

He is also a swim coach in his free time. The coaching lens matters: the same discipline that turns practice into performance shows up in how he builds products, teaches agents, runs teams, evaluates models, and treats people. He was a nationally ranked swimmer, became a network solution architect, lived between Singapore and the United States, and has traveled widely enough to care about how systems work across cultures, incentives, infrastructure, and human defaults.

This.Live is built by pairing human product judgment with agent execution. The goal is a working AI-native software company where projects accumulate memory, improve through receipts, and ship through real verification.
