Cortex Suite · the umbrella

The sovereign agentic system you own.

Today's AI makes you dependent: you rent intelligence from a few providers, your context lives in their clouds, your agents are scattered and forgetful, cost scales with usage, and quality is inconsistent. Cortex is the alternative — a fully-local agentic system you own that is provider-agnostic, token-optimized, remembers locally, and recursively self-improves. The more you use it, the smarter AND cheaper it gets — without anything leaving your machines.

The thesis

A local cost↓ + quality↑ flywheel

Every routed request becomes a label. Every label can train a local specialist. Every specialist routes more work off paid providers and back onto your own hardware. Memory compounds locally instead of being re-bought every session. Use creates leverage instead of recurring cost.
